Hand-Wrapped Heritage

The Essence of Dos Aguas

Founded on the banks of two rivers, our name honors the water that sustains the unique desert-grown cacao of Piura. We are family-run, bean-to-bar makers dedicated to the purist expression of Peruvian heirloom varieties. No fillers, no compromises—just the intoxicating clarity of single-origin excellence.
