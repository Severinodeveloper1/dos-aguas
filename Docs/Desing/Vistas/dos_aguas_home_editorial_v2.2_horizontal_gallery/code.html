OUR TRAJECTORY

From the Peruvian North to NZ Soils

Our journey isn't measured in years, but in the distance traveled by a single bean. We source exclusively from Gran Blanco families, ensuring a trajectory of integrity from harvest to tempering.

"Every bar is a document of the season. A record of rainfall, sun, and soil."

Two Waters, One Soul

Dos Aguas translates to "Two Waters" — a tribute to the lifeblood of our Peruvian family farm and the coastal mist of our Wairarapa studio. We are a bridge between two worlds, united by the pursuit of heirloom cacao perfection. Our roots are deep in the soil of Piura, our hearts are in the craft of New Zealand.
