Returns Policy

As our product is a perishable food item, we generally do not accept returns. However, your satisfaction is our priority. Please contact us within 48 hours of receipt if your order is damaged.

We ship worldwide from our workshop in Mexico City. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and weight. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes upon arrival.
